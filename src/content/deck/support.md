## Deck Support

Deck is built by **Safe Studios**, an independent studio in Amsterdam.

### Contact

For questions, problems or feature requests, email
[philip.mander@gmail.com](mailto:philip.mander@gmail.com).

When reporting a playback or track problem it helps to include:

- Your device and OS version (e.g. Pixel 8 / Android 15, or iPhone 14 / iOS 18)
- What you were doing when the problem happened
- The audio format of the track involved (MP3, FLAC, WAV, ...)

### Common questions

**Where does Deck get music from?**
Deck plays audio files stored on your device. It does not stream, and your
files never leave your phone.

**Does Deck collect my data?**
No. Deck contains no ads, analytics or tracking of any kind. See the
[Privacy Policy](/privacy).

**Is there a manual?**
Yes — the Help page inside the app covers every control, from cueing and
looping to scratch mode and effects.
